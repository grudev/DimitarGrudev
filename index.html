Hi, i'm Dimitar Grudev and i'm passionate all about mobile. All started around 2003 where i did some advertising banners at first then moved to interactive presentations, kiosks, websites and web components/apps. Since 2009 i started to gain some interest in game development and made some simple flash games. Then i landed a job in Favorite Games, well established classic card and board games platform with very big user base. After that in 2011 i started to work in Zariba one of the most succsessful game studios in the region. There i expanded my skills to Unity3D coding with JavaScript, C# and learned about Android and iOS as app and game platforms. Later me and some of my ex-coleagues formed small indie game studio where we decide to try our luck and we produced multiple small games. Alongside with our indie studio i worked on some more freelance projects like mobile calendar app, some trivia games and started to gain more interest and knowledge in native iOS platform. In Feb 2016 i become part of a small but very skillful team of iOS developers in Varna based office of MentorMate. There i could learn and improve my skills further. Now i work on some freelance projects again, for Dentacoin a blockchain company, and Oxeanic a small company with some good established clients. In Oxeanic i work as the solo iOS developer that extends and maintains the mobile app of a small NY based startup.
